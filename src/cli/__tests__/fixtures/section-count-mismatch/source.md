# Optimal Taxation

This lecture covers optimal taxation theory.

## Tax Incidence

The economic incidence of a tax depends on the elasticities of supply and demand,
not on which side of the market is legally required to pay.

## Deadweight Loss

A tax creates deadweight loss proportional to the square of the tax rate:

$$
DWL \approx \frac{1}{2} \eta t^2 Q_0
$$

## Optimal Tax Rate

The optimal tax rate balances revenue needs against efficiency costs.
